Privacy policy

Aura Farm is a private social app for friend groups. This policy explains what information Aura Farm collects, how it is used, and how you can contact us.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and any username or profile information you provide.
  • Circle activity: circle membership, aura nominations, votes, resolved transactions, and invite usage.
  • Technical information: push notification tokens and basic app/device data required to operate the service.

How we use information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To power circles, nominations, voting, leaderboards, and feeds.
  • To send product notifications you enable in the app.
  • To troubleshoot, maintain, and improve Aura Farm.

How circle data works

Aura Farm is built around private circles. Content you create inside a circle, including nominations, votes, and resolved aura events, is visible to members of that circle.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Aura Farm may store a push token so it can send vote requests, resolution updates, join alerts, and other product notifications you enable. You can disable notifications from within the app or through your device settings.

Data sharing

Aura Farm does not sell your personal information. We may use service providers such as hosting, authentication, analytics, email, and push infrastructure to operate the app.

Data retention

We retain data as needed to operate the product, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

Children’s privacy

Aura Farm is not intended for children under 13, or under the minimum age required in your region.
