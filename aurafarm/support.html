Support

Need help?

Aura Farm support is handled directly by Nishil Faldu. If something is broken, confusing, or missing, send a message and include as much detail as you can.

Best way to reach us

Email: nishilfaldu@gmail.com

Helpful details to include

  • Your device type and OS version.
  • Whether the issue is on iPhone or Android.
  • What you were trying to do in the app.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if available.

Account and privacy requests

For privacy, data, or account-related questions, use the same email and mention that your request is related to Aura Farm account data.
